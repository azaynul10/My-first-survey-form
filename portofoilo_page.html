Trophies won by Arsenal FC:

  • Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Islington
  • London, England. Arsenal plays in the Premier League, the top flight of English football.
  • The club has won 13 league titles (including one unbeaten title), a record 14 FA Cups, two League Cups, 16 FA Community Shields, the League Centenary Trophy, one European Cup Winners' Cup, and one Inter-Cities Fairs Cup.
  • In terms of trophies won, it is the third-most successful club in English football.
